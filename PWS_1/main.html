MF DOOM: The Man Behind The Mask

Bio:
Daniel Dumile was born in London in 1971 and relocated to the USA with his family. There, after cutting his teeth on the underground hip-hop with his group KMD and after his brother's tragic death, he coined a new moniker and a new identity for his art, MF DOOM, based on the Marvel villain Doctor Doom.
